## WHAT IS AN EDGE?

An **edge** is a statistical advantage that, when applied consistently, produces positive expectancy over time.

**Components of a Real Edge:**
1. **Historical Proof** — Backtested on 1+ years of data
2. **Statistical Significance** — p-value < 0.05, sample size > 100
3. **Positive Expectancy** — (Win Rate × Avg Win) - (Loss Rate × Avg Loss) > 0
4. **Consistency** — Works across different market conditions
5. **Discipline** — Rules are followed without exception
